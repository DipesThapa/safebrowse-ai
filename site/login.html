What you can manage

After signing in, administrators can configure the full departmental store stack.

  • Inventory & SKU control – track stock, adjust reorder thresholds, and run supplier checks.
  • Billing & POS – reconcile sales, view receipts, and monitor payment integrations.
  • Staff access – invite team members, assign roles (cashier, manager, auditor), and reset credentials.
  • Reports – export daily sales, inventory valuation, and compliance logs for audits.
Secure by default. Sessions expire after inactivity, and two-factor prompts can be added on the backend.
