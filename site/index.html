Quick deployment

Roll out in minutes — whether you manage five devices or five thousand.

  1. Install from the Chrome Web Store or force-install via enterprise policies.
  2. Run through the guided popup tour and upload your first policy blocklist.
  3. Share the hosted privacy and support pages with stakeholders.
  4. Monitor metrics and update rules with the built-in export/import workflow.

Need a detailed checklist? Review docs/DEPLOYMENT.md for packaging, verification, and rollout scripts.
